Description

  • Is a Filipino chain of fast food restaurants owned by Jollibee Foods Corporation.
  • Tony Tan Caktiong and his family opened a Magnolia ice cream parlor in Cubao, Quezon City in 1975. The parlor then began offering hot meals and sandwiches after requests from customers. When the other food items became more popular than ice cream, the family decided to change the ice cream parlor into a fast food restaurant, which was the first Jollibee outlet in 1978.
  • Had a total of about 1,500 Jollibee outlets worldwide as of 2021.
  • Is the largest fast food chain brand in the Philippines.
  • Enjoys the lion's share of the local market that is more than all the other multinational fast food brands in the Philippines combined.
  • The key product of Jollibee is the Chickenjoy, a crispy fried chicken meal, which can be ordered in a number of different options from their menu such as by itself, with rice and gravy, with fries, with buttered corn, or with spaghetti.
  • Commits to serve great tasting food to bring the joy of eating to everyone.
